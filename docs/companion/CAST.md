# Tsumugu — Character bible (recurring cast)

The supplementary readings share a **recurring cast**, so browsing the library feels
like following a series rather than reading a catalog. The cast is **six international
students learning Chinese in Taiwan** (3 male / 3 female — room for drama), plus a
couple of adult anchors and a cat. The premise mirrors the audience directly: every
reader is one of them.

Wedge co-writes the cast; this file is the living canon.
**Status legend:** ✅ locked · ✳️ leaning · ❓ open. Don't finalize ✳️/❓ without Wedge.

**Naming device:** foreigners in Taiwan get a Chinese name on day one (teacher-assigned
or self-picked) — a recurring source of comedy and identity.

---

## Students (3♂ / 3♀)

### ✅ 1 · 沈文 Shěn Wén — the window (♂, Asian-American)
The reader's surrogate: an **ABC who looks the part but can barely speak** — people
launch into fast Chinese and he freezes. He came partly to reach the roots his family
left behind. **His ancestry is deliberately ambiguous — a set-up to pay off later.**
Warm, earnest, wrong-tone-prone. Goes by "Wen."

### ✳️ 2 · 阮草 Nguyễn Thảo — Vietnamese exchange student (♀)
Her superpower is **Hán-Việt**: she keeps realizing she already knows a word
(âm nhạc → 音樂), so vocabulary lands fast and it delights her — this dramatizes the
Vietnamese-rail moat *inside* the story. *(Name may be revisited; the concept is locked.)*

### ❓ 3 · Japanese (gender TBD) — pending
A twist on the stereotype **but not too far** — keep some familiar Japanese flavour
with one surprising turn. Wedge is still choosing the angle. Name device: a Japanese
name is already kanji, simply read in Mandarin.

### ❓ 4 · Indonesian exchange student (♀) — pending
**Not** a hijabi angle. Keep the **name simple** (easy for a brand-new learner to read).
Warm, approachable; the specific angle is open for Wedge to choose.

### ✅ 5 · British — comic + tech (♂)
Sardonic, gadget-and-comics obsessed, bubble-tea addicted, baffled by local social
norms — the comic engine. Running gag: the too-grand Chinese name the teacher stuck
him with, which he can't live up to.

### ✳️ 6 · Local Taiwanese (♀) — but Korea-obsessed
The one **native speaker** in the group (insider guide, culture-bridge, romantic
wildcard) — with the twist that she's a devoted **K-drama / K-pop fan**.

## Adults + motif
- ✅ **蘇老師 Sū Lǎoshī** — the teacher: names them, herds them, and carries the advice +
  the reflective B2–C2 pieces.
- ✳️ **吳老闆 Wú Lǎobǎn** — 麵店 / corner-shop owner; deadpan, feeds the broke ones; the
  neighbourhood's fixed point.
- ✳️ **a ginger cat** — a wordless motif that adopts the whole group; cover-friendly.

## Drama seeds (fuses to light later — Wedge drives)
Three men, three women, all far from home: 沈文 and 阮草 both "have a leg up" while the
visibly-foreign ones don't, which stings; a crush triangle around #6; homesickness
flaring into a fight; the British guy and #6 sparring. Nothing lit yet — just fuses.

## Rules
Original inventions — **never** the ACCC textbook cast (王開文 / 陳月美 / 李明華).
Traditional Chinese; Taiwan-native settings, never costume. Each reading ships with
`title_zh / title_en / title_vi` + a one-line premise (the card hook).
