Design exploration · 2026-07

Six design personalities for the Tsumugu Core library and reader. Five are independent; one descends from the existing silk and seal family. Every mockup sets the same B2 L07 reading and real corpus excerpts, with a Library/Reader tab and a light/dark toggle in its header. Decided 2026-07-02: 冊 Bound Volume wins, refined into the convergence contract (PRD §7 and §8). The shipped outcome: unified/library.html and unified/reader.html in Verdigris green. Green studies: ce-green-studies.html. Record: docs/PRD-Design-Direction-Bound-Volume.md.

  1. 染Rǎn · Resist Dye

    The library is one bolt of indigo cloth that pales as you learn; in resist dyeing the stitched places stay white, so known is literally undyed.

    Look first: card ground-tints on the Reading Wall, then the re-entry wash demo in the reader.

  2. 冊Cè · Bound Volumewinner, 2026-07-02

    Taiwan trade publishing as the register: face-out excerpt cards on shelf rails, a 書腰 obi fact-band in one fixed position, and the Ming-prose experiment.

    Look first: the obi band on any card, then how Noto Serif TC prose reads against the kai incumbents.

  3. 稿紙Gǎozhǐ · Composition Grid

    Measurement wears Taiwan composition paper: coverage as countable character cells, a practice grid that fades as words are learned, prose never gridded.

    Look first: the counted block on a card, the 米字格 in the word popup, and the blackboard dark theme.

  4. 月台Yuètái · Platform

    Taipei wayfinding for self-location: the Companion spine as a route line with you-are-here, lesson codes as station codes, a warm paper reader beyond the signage.

    Look first: the route map, then how completely the signage register stays out of the prose room.

  5. 一盞Yī Zhǎn · One Lamp

    Dark-native evening register: the hub is your next reading at full presence, live and tappable, with the whole library dimmed to a quiet ledger behind it.

    Look first: the opening screen itself, then the hover behavior on the dim ledger rows.

  6. 朱印Zhū Yìn · Seal Ledgerfamily direction

    The existing seal and silk lineage distilled: authored text in print ink, everything your reading wrote in cinnabar, and a chop pressed when a lesson's coverage completes.

    Look first: sort any page's marks into the book's ink versus your ink, then the chop slots on the lesson registry.
